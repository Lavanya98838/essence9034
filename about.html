Our Story

Essence was born from a simple belief: that great food has the power to bring people together. We opened our doors with a vision to create a space where every guest feels genuinely welcomed — not just served.

Nestled in the heart of Brooklyn Heights, our restaurant blends the warmth of rustic decor with the excitement of globally-inspired cuisine. From the very first day, we committed ourselves to sourcing fresh, local, and seasonal ingredients — building lasting relationships with farmers and producers who share our values.

Every dish on our menu is crafted with precision and passion. Whether you're joining us for a quiet weekday lunch or a celebratory dinner, we want your time at Essence to be memorable — a journey of flavours that stays with you long after the meal is over.

What We Stand For
